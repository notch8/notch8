Services

Smooth Ride

Do you have a Rails app in production? Are you worried about keeping it there? Have you been burned by the large overhead for new features because Rails and libraries are updated so fast? With Smooth Ride we spend time every month maintaining your application, providing crucial security patches, updates and responding to emergencies. We reduce growing pains, keep your application secure and get out in front of any scaling issues before they bring your site down or make users unhappy.

Conductor

Keep hearing about all the great things about Rails, but don't know where to get started? Have a Rails team, but seem to be getting farther and farther off track? With Conductor we evaluate processes, make recommendations and work along side you, getting your project written while teaching you the ropes.

Gauge

We provide code and system audits under the Gauge brand. With this product, we take your code and server stack, evaluate it and give you a full report on what is working well for you and what isn't. Whether your just looking for some outside assurances that everything is going smoothly or if you are looking for a technical second opinion, we can turn your code into a readable set metrics and recommendations
